Detailed Description

Chebyshev polynomial representation of a(t).

\[ a(t) = \sum_{n=0}^N a_n T_n(x) \]

where \( T_n(x) \) are Chebyshev polynomials of the first kind and \( x = 2 * (t-t_o)/(t_1-t_0) - 1 \) maps t onto the interval [-1,1].
